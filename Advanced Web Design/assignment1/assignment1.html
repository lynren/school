b. What is the minimum internet speed (connection speed) you may consider as a web designer and why?

As a web designer, I would design for a minimum connection speed of about 1mbps. This covers most internet speeds in the cities and rural parts of North America. It is also compatible with 3G, 4G, DSL, coaxial, and fibre connections. Some sites may require more bandwidth based on the content they offer, making 1mbps hard to design for.
